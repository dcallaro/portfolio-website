01 - Intro

There’s no doubt that saving money is hard to do. A task made even harder when you have multiple bank accounts, and have to visit various apps and websites to merely keep track of your finances.

Breeze understands the headache that savings can cause, and therefore aims to simplify the process, making managing and saving your money easier than ever.

06 - Scenario Usability Testing

To understand how users would engage with the product and complete certain tasks, I conducted a scenario usability test.

The Scenario: You are a new user and would like to create an account and then make a new savings goal.

User feedback was mainly positive and the interface design were well received. There was some confusion surrounding inconsistencies in the live prototype, which have been fixed for in the final product and the only major change made to the UI design was the addition of an onboarding screen to set users up when adding a savings goal.
