The Product

Festivity is a mobile application designed to make private dining an accessible entertainment option for the masses while offering restaurant-style hospitality in the home.

Revolutionizing how chefs engage with consumers, Festivity provides a seamless platform to review, connect, and book chefs instantly. Reservations are as simple as booking a table at your local establishment.

My Role

The creators of Festivity were looking for a UI designer to take the first steps in digitizing and designing the screens for their app, which is still in its initial stages of development. Tasked with bringing the product to life, I set out to create elevated yet practical wireframes that embodied the essence of the service - “premium yet accessible.”

The Starting Block

I was given the following six digital wireframes as a starting point to guide my work, however at a first glance, I knew I needed to take a few steps back to ensure the informational structure and navigation of the app made sense and the final product would include all necessary functions needed for a minimal viable product.
