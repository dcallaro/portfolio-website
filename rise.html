Overview

RISE is a platform for the activist inside each of us, geared to help users discover events and organizations in their local communities - from activist rallies and grassroots events to book clubs and volunteer opportunities.

The Problem

Supporting social, political and environmental causes will always be vital to our society and humanity. On any given day, there are hundreds of events to support different causes and thousands of grassroots organizations already exist, however platforms geared towards discovering these events and orgs. are few and far between.

The Solution

RISE aims to fill that knowledge gap, helping to educate and connect users to the events going on in their backyards and encouraging support and participation for the causes we stand for.
